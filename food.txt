Dolma - stuffed cabbage/grape leaves, often stuffed with meat rice and veggies (popular in places like Greece and Romania)
Biryani - a curry rice with meat 
Makloubi - rice dish with lamb and other ingredients 
Kifteh - a meatball soup
Dikhwa - a barley and yoghurt soup 
Gurgur - a cracked wheat dish 
Tepsi - a baked potato and eggplant dish 
Geymar - clotted cream
*Rooza (Greek loan word) - rice – ܪܘܼܙܵܐ
Shoorwa - ܫܘܼܪܒ݂ܵܐ – soup, stew, broth
